Live Oak Senior Nutrition Center

Good Friends, Good Food, Good Fun!

The Live Oak Senior Nutrition and Service Center provides hot lunches, fellowship, referral and support services in a social setting to adults over the age of 60. Established in 1974 as a ministry of the church, LOSNSC is an important service to the seniors in our community.

Executive Director Kathy Mlinarich and a dedicated team of employees and volunteers join forces to serve weekly lunches.

Daily choices of food include a hot entrée, salad or sandwich. A $3.00 donation is suggested.
