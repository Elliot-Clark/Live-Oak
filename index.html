Live Oak Senior Nutrition Center

Good Friends, Good Food, Good Fun!

The Live Oak Senior Nutrition and Service Center provides hot lunches, fellowship, referral and support services in a social setting to adults over the age of 60. Executive Director Kathy Mlinarich and a dedicated team of employees and volunteers join forces to serve weekly lunches.

Daily choices of food include a hot entrée, salad or sandwich. A $3.00 donation is suggested.

      Mondays, Tuesdays, Thursdays and Fridays:
      Los Gatos United Methodist Church – 111 Church St, Los Gatos

      Wednesdays: Adult Recreation Center
      208 East Main St, Los Gatos

      Other activities include:
      Chair Yoga, Zumba Gold, Blood Pressure checks and fun holiday events.
